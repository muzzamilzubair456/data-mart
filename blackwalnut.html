Full Description

Black Walnut is a luxurious North American hardwood, prized for its rich dark chocolate brown color and fine grain. It is strong yet workable, ideal for furniture, cabinetry, and decorative millwork. Its elegant appearance and stability make it a favorite for high-end interior applications.

Why Black Walnut is special

  • Appearance: Dark brown, straight grain with occasional curly figure.
  • Workability: Machines and sands well; glues and finishes perfectly.
  • Durability: Naturally resistant to decay and insects.
  • High-End Use: Perfect for luxury furniture and cabinetry.

Common Applications

Furniture, Cabinetry, Interior Joinery, Veneers, Millwork, Decorative Panels.

Care & Maintenance

  • Keep dry before installation.
  • Polish periodically to maintain sheen and color.
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to moisture for outdoor use.
