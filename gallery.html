Technical Specifications & Structure

Multi-Layer Protection (Structure)

Our uPVC sheets feature a multi-layered co-extruded design. The top layer is infused with **Anti-UV Stabilizers** for longevity, while the internal layers provide **rigidity and thermal stability** against deflection and expansion.

  • UV Protective Top Layer
  • High-Density Core Layer
  • Color-Fast Base Layer

Available Profiles & Finishes:

  • **Corrugated Sheets:** High load-bearing capacity for large spans.
  • **Trapezoidal Profiles:** Modern design, excellent rain run-off.
  • **Opaque Colors:** Full light blockage for cooler interiors.
  • **Translucent/Clear:** For natural daylight integration (skylights).
