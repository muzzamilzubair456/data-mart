Full Description

Pine Wood is a strong, lightweight softwood commonly used in construction, furniture, and indoor joinery. Its pale yellow tone and straight grain make it versatile and easy to work with. It is cost-effective while offering good strength for structural and decorative uses.

Why Pine Wood is special

  • Appearance: Light yellow, straight grain with occasional knots.
  • Workability: Easy to saw, plane, sand, and glue.
  • Durability: Moderate resistance to decay indoors; treat for outdoor use.
  • Applications: Construction, Furniture, Paneling, Flooring, Joinery.

Common Applications

Framing, Flooring, Furniture, Joinery, Paneling, Decking (with treatment).

Care & Maintenance

  • Store dry and elevated before use.
  • Apply protective coatings if used outdoors.
  • Clean with mild detergent; avoid moisture exposure.
