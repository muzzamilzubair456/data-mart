Cherry Wood

A masterclass in warmth and smooth texture. Cherry wood is celebrated for its rich reddish-brown patina that deepens beautifully with age, making it the premier choice for luxury cabinetry.

Overview

Cherry wood is highly valued for its elegant grain and workable nature. It starts as a light pinkish-brown and matures into a deep, rich red over time, offering a living aesthetic to any interior space.

  • Natural Deepening Color
  • Exceptional Workability
  • Smooth Satin Finish
  • Excellent Carving Quality
  • Consistent Grain Pattern
  • Premium Interior Aesthetic

Primary Applications

  • Luxury Kitchen Cabinetry
  • Fine Heirloom Furniture
  • Architectural Millwork
  • Premium Interior Doors

Care & Origin

Origin: Harvested from sustainable forests across the U.S. and Canada.

Maintenance: To control the darkening process, avoid uneven exposure to direct sunlight. Use natural oils or clear lacquers to highlight the wood’s natural luster.
