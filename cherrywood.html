Full Description

Cherry wood is a highly valued North American hardwood known for its warm reddish-brown color, smooth texture, and naturally deepening tone over time. Its elegant grain, workable nature, and rich finish make it one of the most preferred woods for luxury furniture, cabinets, and premium interior décor.

Why Cherry Wood is Special

  • Appearance: Starts light pinkish-brown and matures into a deep rich red.
  • Workability: Very easy to machine, carve, polish, and finish.
  • Aging: Beautiful natural darkening from sunlight exposure.
  • Premium Use: Ideal for luxury cabinets, tables, and fine furniture.

Common Applications

High-end Furniture, Cabinetry, Interior Joinery, Decorative Panels, Carvings, Premium Doors.

Origin & Sustainability

Care & Maintenance

  • Avoid prolonged direct sunlight to control rapid darkening.
  • Wipe with dry cloth; occasional gentle polishing enhances color.
  • Keep away from excessive moisture.
