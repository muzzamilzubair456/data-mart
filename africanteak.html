Full Description

African Teak is a premium hardwood from West Africa known for its warm golden-brown color, straight grain, and exceptional workability. It is ideal for furniture, cabinetry, flooring, and outdoor structures. Resistant to decay and termites, it offers a balance of strength and aesthetic appeal for high-end projects.

Why African Teak is special

  • Durability: Highly resistant to rot, insect attacks, and weathering.
  • Workability: Machine & hand work easily; holds screws and nails firmly.
  • Finish: Accepts stains, oils, and varnishes beautifully.
  • Color & Grain: Straight to interlocked grain with rich golden hues.

Common Applications

Indoor/Outdoor Furniture, Flooring, Decking, Joinery, Boat Interiors, Luxury Cabinetry.

  • Clean gently with soft cloth or brush.
  • Store in dry, ventilated area before installation.
