About Us
DATA Timber Mart

Data Timber Mart is one of the leading and most trusted suppliers of premium wood and timber products in the region. We specialize in high-quality plywood, solid wood, shuttering wood, commercial boards, MDF, and all types of construction and furniture timber.

Our company has proudly served more than 20,000+ satisfied customers, providing them with reliable material, quick delivery, and consistent quality. With years of experience in the timber industry, we have built a strong network of local and international partners that allows us to offer competitive pricing and excellent product variety.

We also deal in import and export, supplying bulk wooden material to factories, contractors, builders, and retailers. Every product we offer goes through a strict quality-checking process to ensure durability, strength, and long-lasting performance.

Our team works with passion and professionalism to deliver the best support to our clients. Whether it is a small project or a large-scale construction, we provide complete timber solutions under one roof.
