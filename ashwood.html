Full Description

Ash Wood is a light-colored, strong, and elastic hardwood from North America. Its straight grain, shock resistance, and flexibility make it perfect for furniture, sports equipment, flooring, and cabinetry. The wood ages gracefully, maintaining strength and appearance over decades.

Why Ash Wood is special

  • Elasticity: Excellent for items requiring flexibility and impact resistance.
  • Workability: Cuts, planes, and sands easily; accepts finishes well.
  • Appearance: Light creamy color with subtle grain.
  • Durability: Resistant to wear; suitable for furniture and floors.

Common Applications

Furniture, Flooring, Sports Goods (bats, sticks), Interior Joinery, Cabinetry.

  • Clean regularly to avoid dirt accumulation.
  • Apply finish/oil to maintain appearance and durability.
