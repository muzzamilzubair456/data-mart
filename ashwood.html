Overview

Ash is a light-colored, high-density hardwood. Its unique elasticity makes it the premier choice for items that must withstand heavy use without fracturing, ranging from high-end furniture to professional sports equipment.

  • Elasticity for curved joinery
  • High-impact shock resistance
  • Clean, creamy aesthetic tone
  • Decade-long structural integrity

Primary Applications

  • Bespoke Designer Furniture
  • Professional Sports Goods
  • Luxury Interior Joinery
  • Premium Hardwood Flooring

Care & Origin

Origin: Sustainably harvested from North American FSC-certified forests.

Maintenance: To preserve the natural luminescence of the grain, we recommend a light application of natural oils or high-grade varnish.
