Full Description

Red Maranti (Light Red Meranti) is a lightweight tropical hardwood commonly used across Pakistan for doors, frames, furniture, and interior work. It features a soft reddish-brown color with an even texture, making it a cost-effective alternative to premium hardwoods. Its workability and smooth surface finish make it ideal for polished and painted applications.

Why Red Maranti is special

  • Appearance: Light reddish-brown color with fine and even grain.
  • Workability: Easy to cut, shape, sand, and polish.
  • Finishing: Accepts paint, polish, and varnish very well.
  • Applications: Doors, Frames, Furniture, Paneling, Interiors.

Common Applications

Interior Doors, Wardrobes, Frames, Furniture, Ceiling Paneling, Partition Work.
