Sapele Wood

Regarded for its remarkable "Ribbon Grain" and deep mahogany-like luster. Sapele is an exceptionally hard and stable timber, ideal for architectural statements and luxury craftsmanship.

Hardness

~1410 lbf Janka

Density

~670 kg/m³

African
Elegance

Why Sapele is Premium?

Sapele is significantly harder than standard Mahogany. Its defining feature is the interlocking grain which, when quarter-sawn, produces a stunning ribbon-stripe pattern that dances under the light.

  • Iconic Ribbon Stripe Grain
  • Deep Red-Brown Luster
  • Exceptional Hardness
  • High Dimensional Stability
  • Luxury Polish Response
  • Sustainable African Origin

Common Applications

  • Grand Entrance Doors
  • Bespoke Executive Desks
  • Musical Instruments (Guitars)
  • Luxury Yacht Interiors
  • Premium Window Frames
  • High-End Flooring

Care & Handling

Origin: Sourced from Tropical Africa.

Handling: Sapele reacts beautifully to high-gloss polishes and oils which enhance its natural chatoyancy (shine). Due to its hardness, sharp tools are recommended for precision cutting. Store in a humidity-controlled environment to maintain its 8-12% moisture level.
