Full Description

Sapele is a premium African hardwood known for its deep reddish-brown color and ribbon-like grain pattern. It is harder than Mahogany and is widely used for premium doors, furniture, musical instruments, and luxury interior work. Its durability and stunning finish make it highly demanded.

Why Sapele is Premium

  • Ribbon Grain: Unique shine under light.
  • Hard & Durable: Stronger than Mahogany.
  • Excellent Finish: Looks luxurious after polish.
  • Applications: Doors, furniture, instruments.

Common Applications

Doors, Premium Furniture, Panels, Cabinets, Decorative Interiors.
