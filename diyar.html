Full Description

Diyar Wood, also known as Cedar or Deodar, is a premium hardwood from Turkey and Northern Pakistan. It is light brown to golden in color, aromatic, and naturally durable. Widely used in doors, window frames, furniture, and interior constructions, this wood combines strength with beautiful grain patterns, making it ideal for both functional and decorative applications.

Why Diyar is special

  • Aroma: Natural cedar fragrance that repels insects.
  • Workability: Easy to saw, plane, sand, and polish.
  • Durability: Resistant to decay and moderate moisture.
  • Appearance: Smooth grain with warm light brown to golden tones.

Common Applications

Interior & exterior doors, frames, wardrobes, paneling, furniture, and decorative carpentry.

Origin & Sustainability

Sourced from Turkey’s cedar forests and Northern Pakistan’s Deodar forests. FSC certified and legally sourced wood is recommended.

Care & Maintenance

  • Store dry before use.
  • Apply polish or protective coating for outdoor exposure.
  • Clean with mild detergent; avoid excessive moisture.
