General Inquiries

What types of wood do you stock?

We specialize in premium hardwoods including Sapele Wood, Barma Teak, Africa Teak, and Ash Wood, suitable for both residential and commercial projects. We focus on sustainable sourcing.

Do you offer custom cutting services?

Yes, absolutely. We provide precise custom cutting services tailored to your project's exact specifications. You can submit your requirements through our "Get Custom Quote" link.

Shipping & Returns

What is your return policy?

Due to the custom nature of our materials, especially custom-cut and bulk orders, all sales are final. We strongly encourage customers to review samples and specifications carefully before confirming the purchase.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within the region takes 3-5 business days after processing. Custom cuts may require an additional 2-3 days for preparation.
